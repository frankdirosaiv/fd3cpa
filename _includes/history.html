5 Lifestyle Profiles

Early Adulthood

  • Build a Credit Rating
  • Develop Habits of Budgeting, Saving, Investing- Regularly
  • Paying Off any Student Debt
20s-30s

Married Without Children

  • Acquire First Home
  • Adequate Insurance
  • Long Term Investment Emphasis
  • Establish a Will
30s-40s

Married With Children

  • Plan for Childrens' Education
  • Make Sure Insurance is Adequate
  • Update Wills for Changing Family Needs (Name a Guardian)
40s-50s

Nearing Retirement

  • Increase Savings as Family Expenses Decline
  • Layout Specific Retirement Program- Where/When/How?
  • Emphasize Retirement Savings
  • Consider a Trust in Your Estate Plan
  • Reduce Debt in Preparation for Retirement
  • Review Insurance Needs, Especially Medical and Long Term Care
50s-60s

Retirement

  • Downsize
  • Restructure Investments to Produce Income
  • Update Wills and Estate Plans for Changing Family Needs
  • Enjoy Life by Assessing your Sources of Income
60s-70s
